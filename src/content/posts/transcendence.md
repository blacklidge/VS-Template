<p>Two years ago, we were honored to play a part in paving the driveway of the most famous house in our nation. The contractor chose Blacklidge because they knew we would get the job done professionally, quickly and they trusted our American-made product with a proven superior bond. We delivered with our <a href="https://blacklidge.com/ultrafuse/">UltraFuse®</a> product and industry-leading service.</p>
<p>UltraFuse provides the strongest monolithic bond for asphalt surfaces, substantially extending the life of pavements with fast, non-tracking capabilities. So how quickly does it cure and become trackless? Applied at a temperature above 325 degrees Fahrenheit, UltraFuse cures in under 30 seconds and is dry and cool to the touch with a smooth texture and glossy jet-black finish. So, when the US Department of Transportation and the Federal Highway Administration requested Blacklidge’s high-performance, long-lasting solution that does not track, we were proud to answer the call.</p>
<p>Tried and tested is why Blacklidge’s UltraFuse was chosen to pave the way to the White House for a second time. A second contractor chose our product to provide the strongest bond for the asphalt layers on Pennsylvania Ave—directly in front of the White House—along with Jackson Place and Madison Place. Being asked to serve our country for a second time in this unique way is genuinely humbling, and both opportunities are historical projects for our company. See images from both projects below and visit our <a href="https://blacklidge.com/ultrafuse/">UltraFuse page</a> to learn more about one of our high-performance products.</p>
